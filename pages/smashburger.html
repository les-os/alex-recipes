Smashburger

Same smash burger size, but twice the amount of crisp, browned crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 soft hamburger bun
  • 4 oz freshly ground beef chuck, divided in half
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • optional condiments and toppings as desired, such as mayonnaise, mustard, shredded lettuce, onions, tomatoes, and pickles
